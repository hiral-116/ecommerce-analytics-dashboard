## 🎯 Business Objectives

This project answers key business questions:

- Which months generate the highest revenue?
- Which product categories drive sales?
- What payment methods do customers prefer?
- Which brands perform best?
- Who are the most valuable customers?
- How do ratings and reviews impact performance?

## 💡 Key Insights

- Generated **₹7.45M** in revenue from **10,000 orders**.
- **Electronics** was the top category, contributing nearly **60% of total revenue**, but also recorded the highest cancellation rate (**26.1%**).
- **iPhone 14** was the highest revenue-generating product.
- Revenue peaked in **April** and **November**.
- **China** and **France** were the top revenue-generating countries.
- **Adults** generated the highest revenue among all age groups.
- **PayPal** was the leading payment method by revenue.
- The average customer rating was **2.99/5**, indicating room for improvement in customer satisfaction.
- Revenue and customer distribution remained balanced across gender groups.
